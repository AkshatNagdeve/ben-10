Max Tennyson is portrayed as a retired member of an organization known as the Plumbers, a group of intergalactic peacekeepers. He is highly experienced in dealing with extraterrestrial threats and has a vast knowledge of alien technology.

Relationship with Ben: Max acts as a mentor and guide to Ben Tennyson, providing wisdom, support, and guidance as Ben learns to use the Omnitrix, a powerful device that allows him to transform into various alien creatures.

Abilities and Skills: Despite his age, Max Tennyson remains physically fit and capable of handling himself in dangerous situations. He is skilled in combat, tactical planning, and has a knack for improvisation when faced with unexpected challenges.
